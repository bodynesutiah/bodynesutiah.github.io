FAQ#

Q1: What criteria did you use to evaluate the ten candles?#

A1: I assessed each candle based on key factors such as burn time, scent throw, overall ambiance, fragrance profile, and burn characteristics like soot production and tunneling. Additionally, I considered the candle’s design, packaging, and value for money.

Q2: Can you describe the “scent throw” concept?#

A2: Scent throw refers to the ability of a candle to disperse its fragrance throughout a room. It’s influenced by the candle’s wax blend, wick size, and fragrance load. A strong scent throw allows you to enjoy the fragrance even from a distance, while a subtle scent throw creates a more intimate ambiance.

Q3: How did you determine the burn time of each candle?#

A3: I recorded the burn time by continuously monitoring each candle until it was completely extinguished. I ensured that each candle was burned in optimal conditions, such as avoiding drafts and keeping the wick trimmed properly. The burn time information provided in my review reflects the average burn time of multiple candles from each collection.

Q4: What exactly do you mean by “fragrance profile”?#

A4: Fragrance profile refers to the distinct characteristics and notes that make up a candle’s scent. I describe the fragrance profile of each candle by identifying the dominant notes, such as floral, citrus, woody, or spicy. Additionally, I mention any secondary or background notes that contribute to the overall complexity and depth of the fragrance.

Q5: Are the candles you reviewed suitable for people with fragrance sensitivities?#

A5: While some of the candles I reviewed may have stronger scents, others offer more subtle fragrances that might be suitable for individuals with fragrance sensitivities. To ensure compatibility, I recommend reading the product descriptions and reviews carefully before purchasing. Additionally, it’s always advisable to test the candle in a well-ventilated area before using it extensively.
