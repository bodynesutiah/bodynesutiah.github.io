Challenges facing the community and plans for the future#

The Nashville tiny home community is facing several challenges, including:

  • Limited land availability: Finding suitable land for the community to expand has been a significant challenge.
  • Zoning restrictions: Zoning regulations in Nashville restrict the development of tiny homes in certain areas.
  • Lack of affordable housing: The rising cost of housing in Nashville makes it difficult for low-income individuals and families to afford traditional housing, let alone a tiny home.
  • Transportation: The community is located in an area with limited public transportation options, making it difficult for residents to get around without a car.

Despite these challenges, the community has plans for the future, including:

  • Advocating for zoning changes: The community is working with local officials to advocate for zoning changes that would allow for the development of more tiny homes.
  • Partnering with affordable housing organizations: The community is partnering with affordable housing organizations to develop tiny homes for low-income individuals and families.
  • Developing a transportation plan: The community is working to develop a transportation plan that would provide residents with access to public transportation.
