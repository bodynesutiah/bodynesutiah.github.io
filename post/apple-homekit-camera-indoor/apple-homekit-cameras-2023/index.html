Setting Up and Using HomeKit Cameras

  1. Download the Home App: Ensure you have the latest version of the Home app on your iOS device.
  2. Connect Camera to Power: Plug in the camera and place it in the desired location.
  3. Add Camera to HomeKit: Open the Home app, tap the “+” icon, and select “Add Accessory.” Follow the on-screen instructions to connect the camera.
  4. Configure Camera Settings: Once connected, you can adjust various settings, such as motion detection sensitivity and recording preferences.
  5. Remote Access: Access your HomeKit camera remotely from anywhere using the Home app on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac.
